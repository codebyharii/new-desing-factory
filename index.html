ABOUT US

THOUGHTFULLY DESIGNED. BEAUTIFULLY PERSONALISED.

Welcome to The Design Factory — a place where thoughtful design meets meaningful gifting.

Founded in 2019

The Design Factory began with a simple dream: to create beautifully personalised products that bring joy to everyday moments and life's biggest celebrations. What started as a passion for creativity has grown into a trusted destination for premium personalised gifts, stationery, school essentials, drinkware, organisers, and keepsakes loved by families across India.

OUR INSPIRATION

Behind every design is our biggest inspiration — Cappuccino, our beloved four-legged mascot. His playful spirit, unconditional love, and joyful personality remind us to create products that bring happiness to others, one order at a time.

WHY CHOOSE US?

  • Premium quality products
  • Thoughtfully curated designs
  • Beautiful personalisation
  • Perfect for every age and occasion
  • Trusted by thousands across India
  • Made with love and attention to every detail

OUR PROMISE

Every product we create is designed with attention to detail, premium quality, and a personal touch. Whether it's a child's first day at school, a birthday celebration, a thoughtful return gift, or a corporate event, we believe every occasion deserves something unique.

Every order is crafted with care, packed with love, and made to celebrate life's little moments. From your family to ours, thank you for making us a part of your special memories.
